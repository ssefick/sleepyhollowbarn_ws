Common Questions

Details for your planning

Q. What is the guest capacity?

The J.C. Stribling Barn can comfortably accommodate up to 150 guests for a seated reception, and 200 some seated some standing.

Q. Is the barn climate controlled?

Yes! The barn is fully equipped with both heating and air conditioning to ensure comfort during any season in Upstate South Carolina.

Q. Can we use our own catering?

Absolutely. We allow any licensed and insured caterer. We also have a dedicated catering prep area for their use.

Q. What is your alcohol policy?

All events require day of alcohol insurance. You may provide your own alcohol, but it must be served by a licensed and insured bartender. We require security for any event where alcohol is served.

Q. Do you have a space for the bridal party?

Yes, our rental includes access to a private Bridal Suite and a Groomsmen changing area.

Q. Is there on-site parking?

We have ample, lighted on-site parking located just a short walk from the barn and ceremony sites.
